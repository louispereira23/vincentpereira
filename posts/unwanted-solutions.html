Unwanted Solutions

Advice for a better tomorrow often focuses on the past. Yet, in the grand scheme of cosmic time, our lives are incredibly brief. Embrace life as it is without seeking constant solutions.

Don't worry about being labeled good or bad; acknowledge yourself and your desires. Remember that sometimes people seek solutions when there aren't any problems to solve. The influx of unsolicited advise via WhatsApp further exacerbates it, almost compelling one to find a problem.
