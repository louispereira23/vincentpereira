Who am I?

Thought for Today:

Ask yourself, "Who am I?" As you strive to understand yourself, Competing with others loses meaning. Your focus becomes solely on being you.

The challenge lies in self-discovery, For we're often shrouded in falsehood. Yet, beneath the layers, you exist, Unburdened by accolades and attachments, Free of anxiety and at peace within.

Spend time with yourself; it's worthwhile.
